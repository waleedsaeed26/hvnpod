# HVNPOD 90-Day Launch Plan

**Version 1.0 — March 2026**

---

## Overview

This document covers the three-phase 90-day launch plan for HVNPOD: Pre-Launch (Weeks 1–2), Launch Week, and Post-Launch (Weeks 3–12). It also covers the B2B outreach strategy and the metrics and review cadence used to track progress.

The objective is to enter the market with a clear brand identity, generate early demand from coworking operators and corporate buyers, and establish HVNPOD as the reference premium acoustic pod brand in Egypt.

---

### 1.3 Email Waitlist

The waitlist is the most valuable pre-launch asset. Every social channel, every piece of pre-launch content, every outreach email drives here.

| Task | Details |
|---|---|
| Landing page live | Single-purpose: product teaser imagery + waitlist form |
| UTM parameters set | Track source: instagram / linkedin / direct / referral |
| Welcome email automated | Immediate confirmation email, brand voice, what they can expect |
| Lead capture goal | 500 email subscribers by end of Week 2 |

**Waitlist email content:**
- Subject: "You're on the HVNPOD list."
- Body: 3 sentences maximum. Confirm their place. Tell them what's coming. Tell them when to expect it.
- No hollow enthusiasm. No exclamation marks.

---

### 1.4 LinkedIn Pre-Seeding (Thought Leadership)

Before the product reveal, seed LinkedIn with 3 posts that build the problem case. This creates context for the product launch — the audience understands the pain before we introduce the solution.

| Post | Topic | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Post 1 | "The open-plan office was supposed to increase collaboration. Here is what the research actually says." | Week 1, Tuesday |
| Post 2 | "Acoustic privacy is not a luxury. It is a functional requirement. Most offices have none." | Week 1, Thursday |
| Post 3 | "We spent 12 months designing a solution to the office privacy problem. This week, we show it." | Week 2, Wednesday (teaser) |

**Post 3 is the pivot** — it signals a reveal is coming without naming the product. It should generate curiosity and comments.

---

### 1.5 Influencer Outreach

Target 10–15 design and architecture micro-influencers for seeded awareness. Micro is deliberate — 10K–50K following, high engagement, genuine audience in the design and workspace category.

**Target profiles:**
- Interior designers with Egyptian or regional following
- Architecture studio accounts
- Workspace and productivity content creators
- Corporate culture / HR voices on LinkedIn

**Outreach message approach:**
- Personalized, brief — 3 sentences maximum
- Lead with their work ("We've followed your studio's projects closely")
- Offer: early access imagery for preview content, or a scheduled pod visit before/at launch
- No compensation discussed in first message — establish relationship first

**Target:** 5–7 confirmed relationships by end of Week 2.

## Phase 2: Launch Week

**Goal:** Maximum coordinated impact. The market learns about HVNPOD across 5 days with a deliberate product reveal structure that builds momentum each day.

---

### Launch Week Calendar

| Day | Theme | Key Content |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Brand Reveal | "HVNPOD is here" — brand identity announcement |
| Tuesday | HVNPOD One | Full product reveal — specs, story, starting price |
| Wednesday | HVNPOD Duo | Full product reveal — specs, story, starting price |
| Thursday | HVNPOD Meet | Full product reveal — specs, story, starting price |
| Friday | Conversion Push | "Book a Trial" — CTAs across all platforms |
| Saturday–Sunday | Story Content | BTS, team, manufacturing story |

---

### Day 1 — Monday: Brand Reveal

**Theme:** HVNPOD exists. This is what it stands for.

**Content:**
- Instagram: Hero render — the best single image in the portfolio. Caption: brand statement in 2 sentences.
- LinkedIn: Founder post — "We built HVNPOD because [the problem]. Here is what we built." 200–300 words, personal voice.
- X: "HVNPOD. Acoustic privacy pods. Cairo, Egypt. We start today."
- TikTok: Brand reveal video — 30 seconds. Show the pod from outside, open the door, step in. No voice-over needed. Ambient sound.
- Pinterest: Upload full product board — 10–15 renders across all models and finishes.
- Email: Send to waitlist. Subject: "HVNPOD is here." Body: brand reveal, link to website.

---

## Phase 3: Post-Launch (Weeks 3–12)

**Goal:** Convert early attention into documented outcomes. Build the case study library. Seed the next product category.

---

### Weeks 3–4: First Customer Visits and Testimonials

- First batch of trial installations live at target customer sites
- Visit each installation with photographer and videographer
- Collect testimonials: video (preferred) and written
- Document: what changed in the space, how it is being used, what the customer says about it
- Publish: first real-world install content on Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok

**Content output from this period:**
- 3–5 "in-use" photographs per installation
- 1–2 short testimonial clips (30 seconds each)
- One LinkedIn post per installation: factual, outcome-focused

---

### Weeks 5–8: Case Study Content from First Installations

Turn the first installations into structured case studies. These are the most powerful sales tools for B2B buyers — they see their own situation in the story.

**Case study format (LinkedIn article + website page):**

1. The client's situation (anonymized if required)
2. The specific challenge: open-plan noise, lack of private space, call disruption
3. Which HVNPOD models were installed, and where
4. The installation experience: timeline, process, disruption level
5. The outcome: what changed, what feedback was collected
6. One direct quote (with permission)

**Target:** 2–3 published case studies by end of Week 8.

**Social distribution:**
- LinkedIn: Full case study as article + short post driving to article
- Instagram: Before/after carousel + pod-in-context photography
- TikTok: Installation timelapse + customer reaction clip (if available)

---

### Weeks 9–12: Waitlist Campaign for Next Category

HVNPOD's roadmap moves beyond pods. Begin teasing the next product category (smart furniture: desks, panels, seating) to build anticipation and capture a second-wave waitlist.

**Campaign approach:**
- "What comes after the pod?" — teaser content, no product revealed yet
- LinkedIn: founder post on the design philosophy behind HVNPOD's expansion
- Instagram: Material and texture teaser imagery — no product silhouette
- Email: "We are working on what comes next. Be on the list." — separate waitlist capture

**Goal:** 200+ email subscribers to next-category waitlist by end of Week 12.

## B2B Outreach Strategy

Social media builds awareness. Direct outreach builds pipeline. These two tracks run in parallel from Week 1.

---

### Target Segments

| Segment | Title Targets | Outreach Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Coworking spaces | Operations Director, General Manager, Owner | Tier 1 |
| Corporate (50–500 employees) | Facilities Director, HR Manager, Office Manager | Tier 1 |
| Architecture and interior firms | Partner, Lead Designer, Specifier | Tier 2 |
| Hotels and hospitality | General Manager, F&B / Event Manager | Tier 3 |
| Healthcare and professional services | Practice Manager, Office Manager | Tier 3 |

---

### LinkedIn Direct Outreach

**Connection request message (150 characters max):**

> Working on acoustic workspace solutions for [coworking / corporate / design] environments in Cairo — your work at [company] looks relevant.

**Follow-up message after connection (send 3–5 days later):**

> [Name], we just launched HVNPOD — acoustic privacy pods for workspaces, deployed in 48 hours without construction. We are doing trials with our first clients this month. If acoustic privacy is a challenge in your space, worth a conversation.

**Rules:**
- Never paste a product brochure into a first message
- Personalize the connection request with a genuine observation about their company
- Follow-up once only — if no response, move on
- Aim for 20–30 outreach messages per week from launch

---

### Content-Led B2B Strategy

Create content that names their problem and positions HVNPOD as the answer. This earns inbound interest without direct selling.

| Content Type | Example Title | Target Audience |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn article | "How coworking spaces are solving the acoustic privacy problem" | Coworking operators |
| LinkedIn article | "The cost of poor acoustic privacy in a corporate office" | Facilities and HR |
| LinkedIn article | "Why architects are specifying pods instead of building rooms" | Architecture firms |
| Case study post | "One coworking space added 4 HVNPOD Ones. Here is what changed." | All B2B segments |
| Data post | "Research: employees lose [X] hours per week to noise distraction" | HR and facilities |

---

### Events and In-Person

**Target events for HVNPOD presence (Egypt):**

| Event Type | Notes |
|---|---|
| Workspace and facilities management conferences | Attend as exhibitor or sponsor by month 4–6 |
| Architecture and interior design exhibitions | Product presence with a demo unit if feasible |
| Coworking industry meetups | Attend, build relationships, offer exclusive deals for early adopters |
| HR and people management events | Speak on acoustic privacy and workspace wellbeing |

**Demo unit strategy:**
- One HVNPOD One configured as a mobile demo unit — available for client site visits
- Any interested prospect can book a visit; we bring the pod to their office for a day
- This lowers the decision barrier significantly for first-time buyers
